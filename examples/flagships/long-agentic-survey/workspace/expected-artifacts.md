# Expected Artifacts

`maliang init --blueprint long-agentic-survey` creates a workspace whose
`writing/longwrite.yaml` matches this snapshot apart from workspace-specific
identity fields. `writing/malaclaw.yaml` is a derived file regenerated by
`maliang writing sync`; it is intentionally not checked in here.

On a successful flagship release, retain the final manuscript, bibliography,
evidence packets, citation ledger, review records, and
`writing/reports/run-provenance.json`. The derived SQLite index and TeX
intermediates may be archived or safely pruned only after the verified archive
bundle exists.
